Got questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is NeuralPrep completely free?+
Yes! NeuralPrep is 100% free. No signup required for guest access. All features including AI topic suggestions, PDF export and dashboard are completely free.
How accurate are the score predictions?+
Our Linear Regression model is trained on real academic datasets. It provides reliable estimates based on your mid/assignment/attendance scores. Actual results may vary based on final study effort.
Which branches are supported?+
CSE/IT, ECE/EEE, Mechanical, Civil, MBA/MCA and Custom. The Custom option lets any student from any branch add their own subjects freely with AI topic suggestions.
How does the AI topic suggestion work?+
When you click "AI Suggest" on any subject, our backend calls the Google Gemini API which generates the 5 most important exam topics for that subject. You can edit, add or remove suggestions.
How do study reminders work?+
Study reminders use your browser's built-in notification system. In the planner, click "Set Reminder", allow notifications when prompted by your browser, set your time and message. The reminder fires at your chosen time even when the tab is in background.
Is my data saved permanently?+
NeuralPrep uses browser session storage — your data is available during your current browser session. We don't store personal data on servers unless you create an account. You can download your plan as PDF to save it permanently.
